Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 12, 2026

1. Data We Collect

At Spotifree, we collect minimal information to provide a better music experience:

  • Account Info: Your email and name when you sign up.
  • Usage Data: Your listening history (Recent) and Liked songs.
  • Payment Info: Subscription details (processed securely via Firebase).

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to personalize your experience, including:

  • Creating and managing your custom playlists.
  • Providing "Recently Played" history.
  • Processing your Premium subscription status.
  • Sending app updates via the "What's New" section.

3. Data Security

We use **Firebase (Google Cloud)** to securely store your information. Your passwords are encrypted and never stored in plain text. While we strive for 100% security, no method of transmission over the internet is completely foolproof.

4. Your Choices

You have full control over your privacy:

  • You can set your profile to Private in the Settings.
  • You can clear your Recent History at any time.
  • You can delete your account by contacting support.

5. Cookies & Tracking

We use local storage to keep you logged in and remember your volume/player preferences. We do not use third-party tracking cookies for advertising.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the "What's New" section in the app.
